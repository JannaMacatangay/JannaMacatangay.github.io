Goblin Red Door:
Lower Town (Basse-Ville), Quebec City

A nondescript door in the Quebec City neighborhood of Le Petit Champlain has unexpectedly become a hotspot for Asian tourists, who line up to take photos in front of it. This "red magic door" is featured prominently in the popular Korean TV show "Goblin", a fact many locals are unaware of.
