Goblin:
Jumunjin Beach, Gangwon-do Province.

Jumunjin, in Gangwon-do Province, South Korea, is a picturesque seaside town famous for its vibrant atmosphere and iconic filming locations from the K-drama *Goblin* (or *Guardian: The Lonely and Great God*). It offers stunning views that allow fans to immerse themselves in the show's charm and nostalgia.
